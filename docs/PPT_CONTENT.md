## Slide 2: The Problem
**Legacy Systems are Fragmented and Reactive**
- City operators use dozens of disconnected dashboards.
- By the time human operators identify a gridlock, it's already too late.
- Lack of predictive modeling means cities react to disasters instead of preventing them.

## Slide 3: The Solution
**An AI Operations Center, Not a Dashboard**
- **Unified Vision:** Telemetry, traffic, and emergency response in one single glass pane.
- **Predictive AI:** We don't just show data, our AI Neural Pipeline understands it, predicting gridlock 30 minutes before it happens.
- **Procedural 3D Digital Twin:** Code-generated, infinitely scalable visualization of city infrastructure.

## Slide 4: Key Features
**Built for the Enterprise**
- 🧠 **AI Decision Engine:** Continuous Observe -> Analyze -> Predict loop.
- 🚦 **Crisis Simulation:** Run "What If" scenarios (Heavy Rain, VIP Convoy, Major Accident) in a sandbox.
- 📹 **Real-Time IoT Telemetry:** Ingests abstract camera and sensor data from city infrastructure instantly.

## Slide 5: The Architecture
**Decoupled, Fast, and Scalable**
- *Visual: `SYSTEM_DIAGRAM.svg`*
- Next.js 15 App Router & Zustand State.
- Provider Pattern for IoT allows instant hot-swapping from Mock data to real Kafka/MQTT streams.
- React Three Fiber renders procedural environments with zero heavy 3D GLB assets.
