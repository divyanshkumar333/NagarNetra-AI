## Slide 2: The Problem
**Cities Generate Exabytes of Data. We Do Nothing With It.**
- Dashboards are reactive. They tell you a traffic jam *happened*, not how to *prevent* it.
- Human operators are overwhelmed by the volume of IoT telemetry.
- **Result:** Slower emergency response, higher emissions, lost economic productivity.

## Slide 3: The Solution
**An Autonomous AI Operations Center**
- We don't just visualize data; we reason with it.
- A 6-step AI pipeline integrated directly into a 60 FPS 3D Digital Twin.
- Simulates outcomes *before* executing changes in the real world.

## Slide 4: The Core Tech - 3D Engine
**Building a Living City in the Browser**
- **100% Procedural**: No external `.glb` models downloaded.
- **InstancedMesh Rendering**: Tracks thousands of individual vehicles at 60 FPS by bypassing React's render lifecycle and writing directly to WebGL matrix buffers.
- **Cinematic Cameras**: Smooth, damped camera algorithms for enterprise-grade visuals.

## Slide 5: The Core Tech - AI Pipeline
**Transparent Agentic Workflows**
1. **Observe**: Ingests IoT telemetry.
2. **Analyze**: Detects density anomalies.
3. **Predict**: Forecasts cascading downstream gridlock.
4. **Recommend**: Generates mitigations (e.g., adjust signal phases).
5. **Simulate**: Risk-free testing in the Digital Twin.
6. **Execute & Learn**: Updates state and improves future confidence.
