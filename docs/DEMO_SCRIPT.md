# NagarNetra AI: 2-Minute Demo Script

**(0:00 - 0:15) The Hook**
"Hello judges. Welcome to NagarNetra AI, the Intelligent Eye of the City. Cities today rely on fragmented, reactive dashboards. By the time a human operator sees traffic gridlock, it's already too late. NagarNetra isn't a dashboard; it's an AI Operations Copilot."

**(0:15 - 0:45) The Overview**
*(Presenter is on the Dashboard)*
"This is our main Command Center. It aggregates everything: live IoT sensors, 911 dispatch, and traffic cameras. We’ve built a completely procedural 3D Digital Twin of the city—no external 3D models were used; everything you see is generated by code and React Three Fiber."
*(Presenter hits Ctrl+K and navigates to the Digital Twin)*

**(0:45 - 1:30) The Simulation**
*(Presenter goes to Simulation Engine)*
"To show you how our AI works, let's trigger a crisis. We'll start the 'Major Traffic Accident' scenario in Demo Mode."
*(Presenter clicks Start Demo)*
"Watch the timeline on the right. The city starts healthy. Suddenly, a collision occurs during rush hour. Our AI automatically detects the spike in the telemetry layer and triggers the Neural Pipeline."
*(Pause to let the AI animations run)*
"The AI observes the event, predicts the impending gridlock, and simulates a rerouting strategy in the background. It then issues a recommendation to the operator. In demo mode, it auto-approves. You can instantly see traffic flow stabilize as emergency vehicles are routed."

**(1:30 - 2:00) The Close**
"Under the hood, this is powered by Next.js 15 and a heavily decoupled Provider Architecture, meaning this system is ready to plug directly into a city's actual Kafka or MQTT hardware streams tomorrow. NagarNetra AI doesn't just watch the city—it fixes it. Thank you."
