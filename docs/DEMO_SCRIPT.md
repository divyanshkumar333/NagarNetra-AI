# 🎤 2-Minute Hackathon Demo Script

**(0:00 - 0:15) The Hook**
*Speaker:* "Welcome to NagarNetra AI—the Intelligent Eye of the City. Today, smart city dashboards just show you what went wrong *after* it happened. We built an autonomous reasoning engine that detects issues, predicts the fallout, and fixes it before gridlock occurs. And we visualize it all on a live, 60 FPS Digital Twin running entirely in the browser."

**(0:15 - 0:45) The Tech & Architecture**
*Speaker (Gesturing to the screen):* "What you're seeing is thousands of procedural vehicles navigating a spline-based traffic graph using highly optimized InstancedMesh WebGL rendering. No lag, no stuttering. On the left, we have our AI Operations Pipeline."

**(0:45 - 1:15) The Crisis Simulation**
*Speaker:* "Let's trigger a crisis. (Click 'Major Traffic Accident' in the Control Panel). Notice the timeline. Our AI instantly *Observes* the anomaly via mock traffic cameras. It *Analyzes* the density spike, and *Predicts* severe gridlock downtown in 15 minutes."

**(1:15 - 1:45) The Resolution**
*Speaker:* "Now it generates a recommendation. (Click the Recommendation Card). It suggests dynamically altering traffic light phases on the arterial routes and dispatching an emergency unit. We can *Simulate* the fix safely in the Digital Twin. See how the traffic flows differently? Let's *Approve* it."

**(1:45 - 2:00) The Close**
*Speaker:* "The system *Executes* the change, the city health score stabilizes, and the engine *Learns* from the outcome. NagarNetra AI isn't just a dashboard; it's a living, breathing autonomous operator. Thank you."
